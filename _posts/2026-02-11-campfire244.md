The latest Campfire mobile wallet update brings several improvements and fixes focused on Spark functionality and overall performance. 

This update increases Spark mint transaction limits, optimizes Spark spends, improves wallet restore and refresh behavior, resolves multiple Spark and Firo-related address book issues. It also adds better handling of Spark and transparent address selection during swaps. 

You can read the changelogs and download the latest version here: [https://github.com/firoorg/campfire/releases/tag/v2.4.4](https://github.com/firoorg/campfire/releases/tag/v2.4.4)
